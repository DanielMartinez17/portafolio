About us

Hello! We are Mr. Potato and is a pleasure for us that you are visiting our web page to know a little bit more about ourselves. Who are we? We are a restaurant whose food is all potatoes-based; all the delicious snacks and meals we have for you have potatoes as the main ingredient. Our objective is to help the environment and give to our clients a good moment without consuming noxious food, that’s why our beverages are 100% naturals and our restaurant is a place free of technology, with this, we want to provide you a friendly and joyful environment without letting a phone to consume you.

Our history

Who doesn’t love french fries? I think this is an easily question to answer because everybody loves french fries. The idea came up when our team went for a walk to Central Park, “Libertad”, that day all of us were hungry but we only had one dollar on our pockets, and we saw a little street vending that was selling french friesm so we went and ask for some french fries, if it is such a delicious food that everybody likes, then why very few people sell it in a monotonous way? And it was there were the idea came up; then we started to look for different ways and combinations that we could offer to clients until we reached the following results.
